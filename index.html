How It Works

A seamless and intuitive process to empower your Chama with efficiency and transparency.

Register & Approve Members

Register new members, assign roles, update contact details, and approve them through a streamlined verification process to build a trusted Chama community.

Contributions & Transactions

Approved members can make monthly contributions, track payment histories, and manage transactions with full transparency and real-time updates.

Manage Meetings

Schedule and view meetings, join online sessions with one click, and automatically record attendance for efficient and inclusive Chama participation.

Apply & Manage Loans

Apply for loans based on contribution history and registration duration, with streamlined approval, disbursement, and repayment tracking.
